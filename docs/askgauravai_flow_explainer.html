Grid Control · System Flow

How Grid Control runs AskGauravAI

The end-to-end flow we're adopting — from onboarding the brand, to content, to turning that content into leads and paying clients. Every step shows what happens for AskGauravAI and exactly which agent, model, dashboard screen, and approval gate is involved.

Sample brand: @askgauravai · ICP: D2C founders, ecom owners, agencies running Meta ads · Products: Reporting Project ₹2.5–7k → Grid Control ₹30–50k · Generated 9 Jun 2026 · Internal (admin view)
The big picture — two loops, one brain

An agency really runs two connected loops. Grid Control runs both for AskGauravAI, and a single persistent Brain (per-brand memory) keeps the story continuous so nothing restarts cold.

Loop B — Delivery (make the brand grow): research → strategy → content → publish → engage → report → optimize.

Loop A — Growth (turn the audience into money): that engagement becomes DMs → leads → an audit → a sales call → a client.

The one thing that makes this different

Every morning the Chief of Staff (ceo-brain) hands you a single brief: "Here's what the team did overnight, here's what needs your yes/no, here's the one decision only you can make." You don't operate a cockpit — you approve and direct. Research shows 68% of clients churn from lack of proactive guidance; this brief is that fix, built in.

What you actually do vs what the system does

The system handles ~80% of the logistics — research, drafting, scheduling, assembly, reporting. You do the four things only a human should: set the vision, pick the winning angle, approve before anything goes out, and run the sales call. Everything else is the team.
